Welcome to MonoForge

This project provides information about all the weapons available in-game, with a crafting calculator to help determine what materials are needed and how many it required for a specific amount.

Informative

Find out how much damage a specific weapon does, their durability, modes and more.

Crafting Calculator

If an item is craftable, view what skill, level and materials are required to make it!
